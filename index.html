Products

We develop and provide platforms and services within telco and media product ecosystems with strong interplay.

Eon TV

One of the most advanced TV platforms in the region, processing more than 50,000 video assets and consumed through more than 1.5 million devices every day. We believe it changed the way people are used to watching TV by enabling large-scale flexibility. It works with any type of network on cable, fiber, OTT, or mobile. It distributes all kinds of content (on-demand) on any device, location, or time. It is personalized for users, offering them to create profiles, personalized recommendations, and special environments for children and sports mode. EON is available across a market of more than 40 million people.

Eon Connect

A mobile app that helps users manage their household internet connections by letting them configure a set of internet and content connectivity options on their home gateways.

Cometa

A tool used by the Platform Management Department, for editing existing metadata as received from vendors, and the creation of new metadata for EPG and VOD content. Cometa Platform will serve all United Group and the open markets as a metadata aggregator. (e.g. Mediapress)

Gladiator

Anti-piracy platform developed to protect the copyright of multimedia content by monitoring, investigating, collecting, and analysing the evidence of copyright infringement.

Octopus

Intelligent management platform developed to provide essential tools for technical support, service, and developers’ tools for data-driven work. It is a one-stop-shop for telco companies that makes unique value for call centre support.

Chameleon

Single code-based digital media platform that drives some of the most visited news portals in the region like N1 and Nova S that broadcast more than 300 news per day. This is the platform of choice for major journalists and editors across the region with the fastest and most intuitive content publishing workflow. It is scalable, with the infrastructure providing both extreme security and high performance, as it runs on several different servers.

Vida

Addressable advertising platform that combines linear TV with digital advertising targeting and measurement. It enables advertisers and agencies to reach their target groups regardless of what, when, where, or how they are watching television and replaces blocks of advertisements broadcasted for the general population with targeted commercials based on per-viewer behavior.

Leon

Machine learning (ML) platform that enables other applications to use the power of ML technologies in their own business logic. It allows using machine learning and AI in short-form production, advanced content discovery, and personalization.
